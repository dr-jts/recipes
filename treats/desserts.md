## Kulfi (Indian Ice Cream)
* 1 tin condensed milk
* 1 c cream
* 2 c milk
* 2 oz ground almonds
* 2 oz ground pistachios
* dash nutmeg
* few drops vanilla
* Options: ground cardamom, rosewater

1. If using whole nuts use blender to grind nuts (with a bit of milk to form a slurry?)
2. Blend all ingredients
3. Pour into a container and freeze.
4. When partially frozen stir to mix thoroughly
