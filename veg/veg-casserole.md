# Casseroles

## Mushroom-Barley Casserole

* ½ lb Mushrooms, chopped
* 1 lg Onion, chopped
* 1 c Pot Barley
* ¼ c Butter
* ½ t Salt
* ⅛ t Pepper
* 4 c Chicken stock

1. Saute mushrooms, onion and barley in melted butter.  
1. Mix with remaining ingredients in a casserole dish.  
1. Cover and bake for 1 ½ h at 350 F
